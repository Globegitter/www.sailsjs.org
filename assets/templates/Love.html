Built with Love

The Sails framework was developed by Mike McNeil with the support of Balderdash. We build realtime web apps as a service, and after much frustration with the lack of existing solutions, we created Sails to assist us on Node.js projects. Naturally we open-sourced it. We hope it makes your life a little bit easier!
